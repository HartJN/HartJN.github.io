My Learning Plan

Q & A

My long term goal

My ultimate career goal is becoming a full stack software developer.
I aim to be able to develop applications from scratch, from how the site looks, to how the site functions, and everything in-between. I want to be able to join a team of developers and be comfortable with the tasks that I am given.
I believe this is a realistic goal for me, as I understand that all it takes to achieve this is hard-work and consistency. Enspiral Dev Academy will help me achieve this.

My strengths and limitations of learning

My main strength when it comes to learning, is that I love a challenge. From a young age, I have always enjoyed taking things apart and putting them back together to see how they work. When it comes to learning, I find enjoy difficult situations where I have to really think about what I am doing, in order to achieve what I want.

One of my weaknesses is that I can be a bit of a workaholic. It can be an issue, as I can get so involved in my work, that I forgot about other commitments. This is something that I am working on, and will soon have a good understanding about how to balance work and life.

What skills I want to develop

One of the main reasons for me joining Dev Academy, was the range of core skills that they teach.
As my prior programming experience has been self taught, I have not had the experience of learning the human skills needed to become a great developer.
This is my main priority and I am really excited to start this journey and look back at how far I have come, once it is over.

How I will manage my workload

During my time at Dev Academy, I am making it a goal to manage my time well.
I will be using Notion to organise my time and to make sure that I am staying on track.
I also have a whiteboard that I write out my goals for the day and the challenges that I might come across.

Seeking Help

I am well aware of what burnout is, and I will not be putting myself in a position where that could occur. If I am struggling, I will be reaching out to my facilitators and peers for help and guidance.

What I expect from the Facilitation team

I expect a fun and safe learning environment, where everyone is included and feels comfortable seeking help.

Scheduling Information

I am in a position currently, where I can dedicate my time fully to studying at Dev Academy. Things may come up once in awhile, but I will always make sure that it is communicated to my facilitator.
